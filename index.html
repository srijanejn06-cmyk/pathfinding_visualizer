Pathfinding Visualizer (A*)

How to use

  1. Click "Run A*" to see the algorithm find the shortest path (start and end are set by default).
  2. Use "Toggle Wall Mode" and drag on the grid to draw obstacles.
  3. Click "Set Start" or "Set End" to manually place start/end points.
  4. Click "Copy Share URL" or "Share on WhatsApp" to share this demo.
